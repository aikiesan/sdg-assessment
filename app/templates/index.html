Why Use This Tool?

Designed to empower the architectural community, this tool offers a clear path to embedding sustainability into every project, from concept to completion.

Gain Clarity

Understand how your design choices connect to global sustainability targets. Get a structured overview of your project's SDG impact.

Enhance Learning

Access curated information and insights about each SDG's relevance to architecture. Ideal for students and professionals alike.

Broaden Perspective

Think beyond traditional green building metrics. Consider social equity, economic viability, and environmental stewardship holistically.

Simple Steps to Insight

Our intuitive assessment process guides you through evaluating your project's sustainability profile.

1
Define Your Project

Start by providing basic details about your architectural project. Context is key to a relevant assessment.

2
Assess Against SDGs

Respond to targeted questions and criteria for each relevant SDG, reflecting on your design's impact.

3
Review Your Insights

Receive a visual summary and detailed report, highlighting strengths and areas for potential improvement.

4
Learn and Iterate

Use the feedback and resources to inform future design decisions and enhance your sustainable practices.
